ðŸ”¹ 1. Self-Introduction / Background
    
    1. Tell me about yourself.
    2. Walk me through your resume.
    3. Why did you choose this career/job?
    4. What are your strengths?
    5. What are your weaknesses?
    6. How do you handle failure?
    7. How do you handle success?
    8. What motivates you?

ðŸ”¹ 2. Career Goals & Aspirations
    
    9. Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
    10. What are your long-term career goals?
    11. Why do you want to work in this company?
    12. Why are you leaving your current job (or why did you leave your previous role)?
    13. Why did you choose this field/technology?
    14. How do you plan to grow in this company?

ðŸ”¹ 3. Teamwork & Collaboration

    15. Describe a situation where you worked in a team.
    16. How do you handle conflicts in a team?
    17. Have you ever disagreed with your manager? How did you handle it?
    18. How do you handle criticism?
    19. How do you motivate a team member who is underperforming?
    20. How do you handle a difficult colleague?

ðŸ”¹ 4. Leadership & Problem Solving

    21. Describe a situation where you took a leadership role.
    22. How do you prioritize tasks under pressure?
    23. Give an example of a challenging problem you solved.
    24. How do you make decisions when there is ambiguity?
    25. How do you handle tight deadlines?
    26. Tell me about a project where you faced a major obstacle. How did you overcome it?

ðŸ”¹ 5. Work Style & Personal Traits

    27. Are you a detail-oriented person or a big-picture thinker?
    28. How do you handle stress?
    29. How do you manage multiple tasks/projects?
    30. What is your preferred work environment?
    31. Are you comfortable working in a fast-paced environment?
    32. How do you learn new technologies?

ðŸ”¹ 6. Company & Job Fit

    33. Why should we hire you?
    34. What do you know about our company?
    35. How does your background fit this role?
    36. What can you contribute to the team/company?
    37. Are you willing to relocate/travel?
    38. Are you open to working in a different technology/domain?

ðŸ”¹ 7. Behavioral STAR Questions (Situation-Task-Action-Result)

    39. Tell me about a time you failed and what you learned.
    40. Tell me about a time you disagreed with a team member and how you resolved it.
    41. Describe a time you showed initiative.
    42. Describe a time you improved a process.
    43. Describe a situation where you had to adapt quickly.
    44. Give an example of a time you managed a conflict.
    45. Give an example of a time you went above and beyond expectations.

ðŸ”¹ 8. Miscellaneous / Fun HR Questions

    46. What are your hobbies and interests?
    47. If you were an animal, what would you be and why?
    48. How do you spend your weekends?
    49. What book or movie has influenced you the most?
    50. If you could have any superpower, what would it be and why?
    51. How do you handle work-life balance?
